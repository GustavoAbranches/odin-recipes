Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish, combining sheets of pasta with ricotta,
mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, and a savory meat or vegetable sauce. Baked to perfection,
it's a delicious and hearty comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
